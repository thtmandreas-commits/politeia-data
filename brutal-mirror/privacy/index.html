Brutal Mirror Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Summary

Brutal Mirror is a private photo-readiness utility. The current iOS build does not require an account, does not include social sharing, and does not send selfies, photos, face data, camera data, or user content to a third-party AI service.

Photo Use

Photos are used only to provide the check requested by the user. The current build performs its photo check on device using basic image metrics such as lighting, contrast, color balance, and sharpness. Photos are not uploaded by this app and are not retained after the user leaves the result screen.

Accounts

The current build does not require or provide account creation, login, usernames, or passwords.

Advertising and Tracking

The current build does not include a third-party advertising SDK, does not request App Tracking Transparency permission, does not access IDFA, and does not make third-party ad requests. If advertising is added in a future version, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before release.

Data Shared With Third Parties

The current build does not share selfies, photos, face data, camera data, or user content with a third-party AI service. It also does not sell personal data.

Contact

Questions can be sent to contact@politeiaproject.org.
